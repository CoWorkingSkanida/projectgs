Facilities

Homestay with Fantastic Nature View

A calm and comfortable atmosphere are the right words to describe each room There is. Griya Sejahtera provides comfortable mattresses accompanied by pillows and blankets for maximize your rest.

Camping Ground

Playing in nature is incomplete without camping! Eiits calm down, Griya Sejahtera also provides Camping Ground for an increasingly enjoyable experience with nature. Large tents are available (6 people) and regular size tents (4 people) are equipped with sleeping bags.

BBQ Night

While enjoying the cool night at Griya Sejahtera which is surrounded by six (6) mountains for sure will be more complete with a BBQ atmosphere with family, friends, and relatives. Griya Sejahtera prepares corn and bread along with tools and the ingredients needed for BBQ!

Breakfast

Early in the morning, drinking warm while snacking on brown bread is a special pleasure that can be felt at Griya Sejahtera! Besides wedang, there is also coffee that can be enjoyed. Breakfast at Griya Sejahtera can be enjoyed while sitting in the dining room, a walk in the parking area, or in the tent arena.
